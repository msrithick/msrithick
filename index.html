About us
The Ayya Nadar Janaki Ammal College established in 1963 is a standing testimony to the wisdom and farsighted of The Legendary of Humanitarianism Thiru. P. Ayya Nadar, a farsighted industrial architect of Sivakasi town and renowned academician and his equally generous wife Thirumathi A. Janaki Ammal, caters for the need of the rural sector for higher education. As this Institution was established by their Munificence, the College was fittingly named after them. Discipline and Academic Excellence are Hall Mark of this Institution.
